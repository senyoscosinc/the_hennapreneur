Frequently Asked Questions

How long does henna last?

Henna typically lasts 1-3 weeks depending on the body part and aftercare. Hands usually show the deepest color and last the longest.

What's your cancellation policy?

You can cancel or reschedule up to 24 hours before your booking without charge. Cancellations within 24 hours may incur a 50% fee.

Do you offer custom designs?

Yes! We offer custom henna designs tailored to your preferences. Please reach out with your ideas and we'll create something beautiful for you.

Are your products organic?

All our henna products are made from 100% natural, organic ingredients with no harmful chemicals or additives. They're safe for all skin types.

How do I enroll in the online course?

Visit our course section and click "Sign Up Now". You'll receive access to all course materials immediately after enrollment.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, PayPal, and bank transfers. All payments are secure and encrypted.
